direct object

who we are

direct object is a book. it is a collection of the ways we all individually react to a central idea. it is a frame for these reactionary objects – your objects – to cohabitate. it lives in print and online.

how we work

we will provide the central idea, the catalyst for each issue. think of your submissions as objects – artifacts of your reaction to the idea. they should be literal, absolute, exact. the only lens is your perspective. a hand is a hand is a hand.

each submission should fit within the frame of a single page. we accept any media, but please limit submissions to “one” – one photo, one hundred words of text, one piece of mail, one minute of video, one fingernail clipping.

in the same way a window provides clarity but also a reflection, the lenses will be compiled to present a composite view of a singular idea.
